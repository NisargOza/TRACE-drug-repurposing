IMPORTANT CAVEAT — ILD-inclusive dataset:
  GSE47460 contains ALL ILD subtypes, not pure IPF. The 194 ILD samples include:
    - 122 with confirmed UIP/IPF subtype ("ild subtype: 2-UIP/IPF" in characteristics)
    - ~72 with other ILD subtypes (NSIP, DIP, other) or insufficient subtype annotation
  The 77% concordance was computed on all ILD vs. controls. If restricted to
  the 122 UIP/IPF samples only, a higher concordance would be expected since
  the consensus signature was built from IPF cases. The 77% figure is therefore
  a CONSERVATIVE estimate (ILD-inclusive makes the test harder, not easier).

  Subtype-restricted analysis (IPF/UIP only vs. controls) is the cleaner
  comparison and is the recommended next iteration of this validation.
