Interpretation:
  INCONCLUSIVE (no-control limitation): GSE134692 has no healthy controls, so
  neither z-score correlation nor ssGSEA can separate 'up in IPF vs. control'
  from 'low-expressing gene.' Consensus-UP genes (e.g. KRT5, S100A2) have low
  absolute expression even in IPF; consensus-DOWN genes (e.g. SFTPC, ABCA3) are
  among the highest-expressed genes in any lung tissue. This produces the observed
  negative correlation and low UP-gene ssGSEA score — it is an artifact of the
  no-control design, not evidence against the consensus signature.

  REPORTABLE FINDING: Proper held-out validation requires a dataset with both
  IPF and healthy lung controls (e.g. a split of one training dataset or an
  independent case/control series not yet used). GSE134692 is better used as
  a consistency check on gene variance, not direction.
